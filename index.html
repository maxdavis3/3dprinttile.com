Custom Tile Services

Architectural Facade Tiles

Large-format facade tiles with parametric relief patterns. Interlocking geometry, depth variation across a run, and custom glaze programs. Fire-rated and frost-resistant compositions for exterior application.

02

Interior Feature Walls

Interior tile collections with controlled surface relief — from subtle texture to dramatic 3D form. Coordinate across grout line, edge profile, and finish to create cohesive wall installations without repetition.

03

Designer Floor Tiles

Slip-rated floor tile in pressed ceramic and porcelain body. Topographic patterns, inlay effects, and directional texture in any plan size. Non-porous, stain-resistant glaze surfaces for commercial traffic.

MATERIALS

Tile Material & Finish Options

Every tile is fired ceramic — not resin, not polymer. Permanent colour-through body, food-safe glazes, and structural integrity that outlasts the building.

  • ● Porcelain body — frost-resistant, <0.1% water absorption
  • ● Terracotta clay — warm tones for residential interiors
  • ● Stoneware body — mid-fire, natural matte aesthetics
  • ● Reactive glaze program — unique variation across each piece
  • ● Matte, satin, and gloss glaze options per project
  • ● Custom RAL or Pantone glaze color matching available
